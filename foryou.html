My Everything

My love,

Every time I see you, the world slows down for a moment,
as if the universe is reminding me how lucky I am to love you.
You still give me butterflies,
and I hope I never get used to this beautiful feeling.

In this endless galaxy of stars,
my eyes will always find you first.
You are my peace in every storm, my comfort in every silence,
and the place my heart will always call home.

Thank you for choosing me, loving me, and staying by my side.
If I had to live this life again, I would still search for you in every universe.
I will keep choosing you - today, tomorrow, and every day after that.

I love you more than words can explain,
more than the stars, more than forever itself.
You are my everything - my always, my forever. ❤️🌌
